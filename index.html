Why This Seminar?

Prayer is one of the core rhythms of the Christian life, yet many of us feel dissatisfied or disconnected in it.

What if your struggle with prayer is not a problem to fix, but an invitation to deepen your relationship with Jesus?

The A Praying Life Seminar is designed to foster this relationship through practical biblical teaching and guided prayer training.
