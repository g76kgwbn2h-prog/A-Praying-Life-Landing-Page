A Praying Life Seminar

Foster your relationship with Jesus through prayer.

What if your struggle with prayer is not a problem to fix, but an invitation to deepen your relationship with Jesus?

A Praying Life Seminar is designed to foster this relationship through practical biblical teaching and training in prayer.

Through meaningful teaching and opportunities to pray together, this seminar equips you and your church to move toward a richer life with Jesus.

Working with Churches

The A Praying Life Seminar is ideal for your local church.

Whether your church is ready to host this seminar or your leaders want additional training, A Praying Life exists to support you.

Our heart is to help whole congregations become communities that pray in dependence on Jesus.
